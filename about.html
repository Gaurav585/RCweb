About RCKCCEMSR

Welcome to RCKCCEMSR

The Rotaract Club at K.C. College of Engineering and Management Studies and Research aims to provide students with opportunities for personal and professional development through community service, leadership training, and networking. Its objectives include promoting goodwill and understanding among people, fostering ethical leadership, and contributing to the betterment of society.
